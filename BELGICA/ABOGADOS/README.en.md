# LEGAL AID AND SOCIAL ASSISTANCE ORGANIZATIONS

### MYRIA
Legal information and advice on:
- Family reunification procedures
- Municipal registration
- Detention centres
- Return and repatriation
- Investigation of sham marriages or false civil partnerships
- Access to Belgian nationality and statelessness
- Filing complaints with the police and social inspection services for undocumented migrants
- **Recovery of unpaid wages for undocumented workers**

- Phone: 0800 14912 (free)
- Hours: Mondays and Thursdays, 09:30 – 12:30
- Email: myria@myria.be
- Address: Place Victor Horta 40, box 40, 1060 Saint-Gilles (Brussels)
- Website: https://www.myria.be/en
- Contact form: https://www.myria.be/en/contact-us
