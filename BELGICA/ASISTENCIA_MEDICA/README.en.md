## WHAT ARE MY RIGHTS WHEN REQUESTING INTERNATIONAL PROTECTION WITH FEDASIL?

While your asylum application is being processed — even if you are not living in a reception centre — Fedasil remains responsible for covering most of your medical costs.

Before any medical appointment, you must contact Fedasil's medical service. Fedasil must confirm it will cover the costs **before** you visit the doctor. This confirmation is called the **Requisitorium**.

### HOW TO OBTAIN THE REQUISITORIUM

Send an email to Fedasil's medical service.

**FEDASIL — Medical Costs Centralisation Unit**
- Address: Kartuizersstraat 21, 1000 Brussels
- Website: https://www.fedasil.be/en
- Hours: Monday to Friday, 09:00 – 12:00
- Phone (NL): 02 213 43 00
- Phone (FR): 02 213 43 25
- Email: medic@fedasil.be
- Online form: https://www.fedasil.be/en/form/webform-11190
- PDF form: https://medimmigrant.be/IMG/pdf/requisitorium_form_nl.pdf *(includes added translation)*

Fedasil will send the Requisitorium to your email address. Hand it to your doctor. After the appointment, the doctor, hospital, or specialist will return the form to Fedasil — you do not have to pay.


## EMERGENCIES

In case of a medical emergency, go directly to any hospital.

**CALL `112` FOR AN AMBULANCE**

Do not forget to request:
- (Dutch) *attest van spoedeisende zorgen*
- (French) *attestation des soins urgents*

This document proves that an asylum seeker has received emergency medical care and that Fedasil must cover the costs. Send it along with your invoice to Fedasil at medic@fedasil.be.
