# LEGAL AID AND SOCIAL ASSISTANCE ORGANIZATIONS

### PRO ASYL
Defends the rights of refugees and asylum seekers. Provides individual legal advice and representation up to the Federal Constitutional Court or the European Court of Human Rights if necessary.

- Website: https://www.proasyl.de/en/
- Advice in individual cases: https://www.proasyl.de/en/pro-asyl-advice-in-individual-cases/
- Network of refugee councils across Germany: https://www.proasyl.de/en/help-and-advice/refugee-councils/


### KuB — Kontakt- und Beratungsstelle für Geflüchtete und Migrant*innen
Contact and advice centre for refugees and migrants in Berlin-Kreuzberg. Offers asylum and residence law advice, psychosocial support, German language courses, and social accompaniment. Serves in German, English, French, **Spanish**, Arabic, Farsi, and Russian.

- Address: Oranienstr. 159, 10969 Berlin-Kreuzberg (Metro U8 Moritzplatz)
- Phone: 030 614 94 00
- Email: kontakt@kub-berlin.org
- Website: https://kub-berlin.org/en/
- Asylum/residence advice: Mon, Tue, Thu, Fri 09:00–13:00 (walk-in)
- Social counselling: Mon & Thu 14:00–17:00 (walk-in)
- Phone availability: Mon & Thu 14:00–17:00


### CARITAS GERMANY
Social, administrative, and advisory assistance for migrants and refugees. Present across Germany.

- National website: https://www.caritas.de
- Directory of migrant advice centres: https://www.caritas.de/hilfeundberatung/ratgeber/migration


### DIAKONIE
Social services network with migrant advice centres across Germany, including legal, social, and psychological support.

- Website: https://www.diakonie.de
- Migrant counselling directory: https://www.diakonie.de/hilfe-und-beratung/migrantenberatung


### AWO — Arbeiterwohlfahrt
Social welfare and integration services for immigrants and refugees, with centres nationwide.

- Website: https://www.awo.org
- Migrant advisory services: https://www.awo.org/thema/migration-und-integration


### REFUGEE ADVICE CENTRE NETWORK (Asyl.net)
Comprehensive directory of legal and social advice centres for asylum seekers across Germany.

- Website: https://adressen.asyl.net/


### UNHCR GERMANY
- Website: https://help.unhcr.org/germany/
- Information on the asylum procedure, rights, and available services in Germany.
