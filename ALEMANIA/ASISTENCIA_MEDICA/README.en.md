# FREE MEDICAL ASSISTANCE IN GERMANY

## MEDICAL RIGHTS BY IMMIGRATION STATUS

- **Asylum seekers (first 15 months):** Emergency and acute medical care covered under the *Asylbewerberleistungsgesetz* (Asylum Seekers' Benefits Act). Authorisation must be obtained through the social welfare office (*Sozialamt*) of your federal state.
- **Asylum seekers (after 15 months):** Access to the public health system equivalent to German citizens.
- **Undocumented people:** No right to the public system, but can access the free and anonymous clinics listed below.

## FREE AND ANONYMOUS CLINICS

**Malteser Migranten Medizin — Berlin**
- Phone: 030 82 72 26 00
- Email: mmm.berlin@malteser.org
- Website: https://www.malteser.de/standorte/berlin/medizin-fuer-menschen-ohne-krankenversicherung.html
- Free and anonymous care for people without health insurance: undocumented migrants, rejected asylum seekers, homeless people, seasonal workers.
- Network of more than 20 clinics across Germany.

## EMERGENCIES

**CALL `112` FOR AN AMBULANCE**

In an emergency, any hospital is obliged to treat you regardless of your immigration status.
