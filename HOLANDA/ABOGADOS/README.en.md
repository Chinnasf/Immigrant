# LEGAL AID AND SOCIAL ASSISTANCE ORGANIZATIONS

### VLUCHTELINGENWERK NEDERLAND — Dutch Council for Refugees
Defends the rights of refugees and asylum seekers in the Netherlands. Offers information on procedures, legal advice on asylum and family reunification, and personal support. Available in 11 languages.

- Website: https://www.vluchtelingenwerk.nl/en
- Information for refugees: https://www.refugeehelp.nl/


### ASKV — Refugee Support Amsterdam
Legal assistance and social support for rejected asylum seekers in the Netherlands.

- Website: https://www.askv.nl/en/


### VLUCHTELINGEN IN DE KNEL
Supports non-European asylum seekers whose applications have been rejected, defending their basic rights: food, clothing, medical care, and access to the legal system.

- Website: https://vluchtelingenindeknelnederland.nl/


### LOKET ONGEDOCUMENTEERDEN — Amsterdam *(Office for Undocumented People)*
Amsterdam municipal office for people in irregular situations. Offers information, advice, and access to municipal services such as 24-hour emergency shelter, pocket money, and medical screening.

- Website: https://www.amsterdam.nl/en/refugees/provisions-asylum/


### COA — Central Agency for the Reception of Asylum Seekers
Dutch government body responsible for accommodation and guidance during the asylum procedure.

- Website: https://www.coa.nl/en


### UNHCR NETHERLANDS
- Website: https://help.unhcr.org/netherlands/
- Information on the asylum procedure, rights, services, and support organisations in the Netherlands.
